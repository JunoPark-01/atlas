Genshin Instrumentals

Although within most of the gaming communities the ambience/background music may not always be the first thing people gravitate to a game for. However, within a particular game called Genshin Impact, their attention to what instruments are played within different locations, the music of places where they are inspired by real life examples, and yet their ability to adapt such cultural music to the modern world to experience. All this, if not most of the credit can be attributed to Yu-Peng Chen alongside Mihoyo's(Creators of Genshin Impact) own music creation team Hoyo-mix.
